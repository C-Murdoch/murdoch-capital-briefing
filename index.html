Key Themes for Today

The dominant story for investors today is the compounding pressure of a Strait of Hormuz-driven energy shock layered on top of a divided, transitioning Federal Reserve - Brent crude at US$120+ is simultaneously stoking inflation concerns, lifting bond yields, and weighing on equities, while Powell's imminent departure removes a known policy anchor at exactly the wrong moment. Within this macro backdrop, private equity activity is quietly accelerating, with the Carlyle-MAI and Blackstone-Senior deals illustrating that well-capitalised sponsors are deploying into dislocation; for equity markets, the next key catalyst will be clarity on the Iran-US negotiations and the identity of the next Fed Chair.
