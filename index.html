Key Themes for Today

The dominant story is the fragile US-Iran ceasefire, which has injected sudden volatility into oil markets and a sharp relief rally into equities - but with Strait of Hormuz traffic still disrupted and Israeli military activity continuing in Lebanon, the risk of a rapid reversal is significant. Against this backdrop, investors must also contend with a worsening inflation outlook in the US, where tariff pass-through is accelerating just as the Fed signals limited appetite for further cuts - creating a challenging environment where geopolitical de-escalation and monetary policy are pulling in opposite directions.
