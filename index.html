Key Themes for Today

Markets are in a tentative risk-on mood, driven entirely by reports that the US-Iran conflict may be approaching a resolution - a development that, if confirmed, would remove the primary upside inflation risk that has kept central banks on hold and weighed on growth outlooks globally. However, the simultaneous rally in both equities and gold suggests investors remain sceptical of a clean ceasefire, and any reversal in geopolitical news could quickly unwind today's gains. With the Fed, ECB, and BoE all holding rates steady and inflation still running above target, the path to rate cuts in 2026 remains narrow and data-dependent.
