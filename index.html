1. Global Macro

Key Themes for Today

The dominant theme today is the tension between a geopolitically-driven relief rally in risk assets and persistently elevated inflation - particularly in the U.S., where March CPI surged to 3.3% on the back of a 21% spike in gasoline prices. Central banks remain in a holding pattern, with rate cuts increasingly priced out for 2026, meaning any renewed escalation in the Middle East conflict could quickly reverse the cautious optimism now supporting equities and the AUD. For private markets, record fundraising (KKR's $23B close) and a pipeline of large-cap deals signal that institutional capital is deploying despite macro headwinds.
