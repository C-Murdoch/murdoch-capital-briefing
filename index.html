Key Themes for Today

The dominant macro story remains the interaction between elevated energy prices - driven by Middle East conflict - and the policy response from central banks, with the RBA and Fed both signalling that the last mile of the inflation fight is proving stubborn. Meanwhile, equity markets are repricing for a "higher for longer" regime that markets had too quickly dismissed earlier this year, and the unwinding of tariff uncertainty (via the Supreme Court ruling) has provided a degree of relief without fully removing trade policy risk as a drag on business investment and deal-making.
