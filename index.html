Key Themes for Today

The dominant theme for investors today is the re-escalation of US-Iran tensions and its cascading effects: an oil price spike feeding into already-elevated headline inflation (US CPI at 3.3%), putting the Fed in a difficult position ahead of its April 30 decision. With equities softening, gold consolidating, and energy stocks outperforming, markets are repricing geopolitical risk premium across asset classes. Private capital markets remain broadly constructive - deal activity is resilient, dry powder is ample, and notable transactions continue to close - but macro uncertainty is likely to delay some larger processes until there is greater clarity on the oil and rates outlook.
