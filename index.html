Key Themes for Today

Global markets are navigating a tense intersection of geopolitical risk and monetary policy divergence: Brent crude above $100, gold at record highs, and central banks - both the Fed and ECB - holding rates while signalling caution, all point to a macro environment where inflation and uncertainty remain stubbornly elevated. The temporary US-Iran de-escalation has provided a short-term reprieve for equities, but with the ASX and S&P 500 both in meaningful drawdowns from recent highs, the underlying vulnerability of risk assets is increasingly evident. Meanwhile, the record Q1 M&A surge driven by mega-deals and PE dry powder deployment suggests that deal activity is accelerating into this volatility, with AI infrastructure remaining the dominant structural theme attracting capital.
