1. Global Macro

Key Themes for Today

Geopolitical risk surrounding the Iran conflict remains the defining variable for markets: it is simultaneously driving energy inflation higher (pressuring central banks to stay tight or tighten further, as seen with the RBA), dampening risk appetite in equities, and shaping capital flows in commodity and FX markets. Early diplomatic signals from the US-Iran front are providing intermittent relief to gold and oil, but the underlying uncertainty continues to make investors cautious and is increasingly feeding into second-round inflationary effects globally.
