Key Themes for Today

The dominant cross-asset story is the feedback loop running from Middle East risk into oil, into inflation expectations, and back into central bank reaction functions. With the Fed effectively sidelined and the RBA's June meeting genuinely live, sensitivity to energy and FX moves is unusually high. At the same time, private capital continues to lean into the largest, highest-conviction deals, suggesting that beneath the macro noise, dry powder is being deployed selectively rather than withheld.
