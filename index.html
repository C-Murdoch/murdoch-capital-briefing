Key Themes for Today

The dominant cross-cutting theme remains the Middle East oil shock: elevated energy prices are simultaneously pushing inflation higher across major economies (US CPI at 3.8%, RBA hiking for a third time), constraining central bank easing cycles, and keeping commodity markets tight - creating a stagflationary undertow that equity markets are, for now, choosing to look through on the back of AI and earnings momentum. For investors, the divergence between resilient risk assets and deteriorating macro fundamentals is the key tension to monitor heading into the June central bank calendar.
